Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese might appear simple, but it's an incredibly efficient use of few materials to make something creamy and moist inducing.
If for some reason you don't salivate over the thought of this meal, do consult your GP as soon as possible.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • White sauce
  • Lots of cheese
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Get all the ingredients together
  2. Prepare & where necessary, cook them
  3. Voila, you have food
  4. Eat the food
